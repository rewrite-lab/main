04. Alliance

Support & FAQ

Is Rewrite Lab a for-profit security company?
No. We are not a profit-driven company. Rewrite Lab was founded by top web hackers from around the world who voluntarily came together to push the limits of security research and transparently share that knowledge with the community. We are a pure research lab.
How are sponsorship funds used?
Every contribution is used transparently to support high-quality research content, maintenance of our public wargame infrastructure, and seminars and conferences that help grow the global web hacking community, building a sustainable cycle of knowledge sharing.
Do you recruit team members on a rolling basis?
We do not recruit on a rolling basis. In general, we open recruiting twice a year, once in the first half and once later in the year. We are not currently recruiting additional researchers.
